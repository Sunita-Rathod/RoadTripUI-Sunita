Mumbai, one of the major cosmopolitan cities in India, is a popular tourist attraction. It is primarily famous for large markets, mesmerising beaches, towering buildings, and exquisite five-star hotels.

The Gateway of India, the historical arch-monument, is what Mumbai is famous for primarily. It is a large basalt-made monument, which served as a ceremonial place during the British rule to welcome King George V in India in 191
